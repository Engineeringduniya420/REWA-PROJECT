"REWA" THE CITY OF WHITE TIGER

Rewa is a city in north-eastern part of Madhya Pradesh state in India.

Rewa is famous for many unique things which has rightfully earned her a place amongst sought after places of Madhya Pradesh . But the one distinction that has given her immense fame and also unique identity is white tiger - one of the rarest species of Tiger. For being home to huge number of such rare species of tigers, today Rewa is proudly referred as - - -

"Land of White Tigers".
